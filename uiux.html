About Ui / Ux


The visual elements of a product or service, such as buttons, icons, screens, and layout. UI should be easy to use and aesthetically pleasing.

The overall experience a user has with a product, including their initial impression and final outcome. UX design focuses on the feel of a product and how it will lead to a meaningful experience for users

A UX designer - or user experience designer - has one objective in mind: making interacting with products, websites, and apps as smooth, efficient, and enjoyable as possible for their users.
